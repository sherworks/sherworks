Digital Checkout Experience

Onboarding flow taking too longer, complicated checkout process, and buggy applications can all turn customer away and contribute to a low order completion rate.

Roles:
UX & UI Designer

Skills:
Figma, UX research

Goals:
Faster checkout & onboarding, relocation development team effort, and efficient integration.

Problems

Slow onboarding, bug reports, unstable codebase, low checkout ratio, high engineering cost, difficult integration and feature improvement.

Rearch & Solution

Inorder to improve the flow of placing an order, the experience should be identical and consistent across platforms. If in store order, web order, mobile order, and phone order are all following a consistent flow, users would be less likely to get confused during the process.
